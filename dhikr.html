Dhikr & Dua

Dhikr (remembrance of Allah) and Dua (supplication) are fundamental practices in Islam that strengthen our connection with Allah and bring peace to our hearts. Engaging in regular Dhikr and making sincere Dua are means of attaining spiritual tranquility and blessings.

We recommend using the Dhikr & Dua App, a comprehensive resource that offers a wide range of supplications and remembrances for daily use.
